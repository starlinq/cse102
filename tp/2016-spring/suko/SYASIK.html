1. 노랑통닭 - Yellow Chicken

Address:Incheon-si Yeonsu-gu Street of Singsong 125-13 Songdo Arcria1 188

Phone:032-832-2292

Open-time: Everyday 14:00~2:00

Menu/Price:
후라이드치킨 (Fried chicken) - 16,000 won

양념치킨 (Spicy chicken) - 17,000 won

감자튀김 (French Fries) - 7,000 won

About 노랑통닭(Yellow Chicken):This is one of famous chicken restaurant in Songdo.
As you can imagine when you hear the name of restaurant, they make the chicken with some carry.
But, you never taste the carry on the chicken which is the unique thing why so many people like this restaurant.
And they give pretty big amount of chicken which is almost for 3 people.
