Bunsik in Gang-Nam

The Gangnam District is one of the 25 gu which make up the city of Seoul, South Korea.
There are many delicious restaurants in Gang-nam as it is the most developed region in Korea.
We would like to introduce to you about two famous Korean BBQ restaurants in Gangnam.

1.슈퍼집 - Superzip

Address:14, Eonju-ro 130-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:02-540-1591

Open-time: Everyday 23:00 ~ 03:00

Menu/Price:

Superzip ddukbokki(떡볶이) - 10,000won

Little gimbap(꼬마김밥) - 3,000won

Variety of fritter - 10,000won

About 슈퍼집 (Superzip):This restaurant is so famous in Gangnam that many Korean celebrities come to
this restaurant for bunsik and mid-night snacks.This bunsik restaurant has been on a famous
food channel called "Tastyroad" as well.


2.공수간 - Gongsugan

Address:814-5, Yeoksam-dong, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:02-562-0414

Open-time: Everyday 11:00~23:00

Menu/Price:

Ddukbokki with soup - 3,000won

Quick Gongsugan Ddukbokki(2people) - 10,000won

Carbonara Quick Gongsugan Ddukbokki(2people) - 15,000won

About 공수간(Gongsugan):This place provides ddukbokki on the spot.
Because it is served right away when they customer orders, all menus are freshly served.
