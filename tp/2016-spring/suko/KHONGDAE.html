Korean BBQ in Hong-Dae

There are many Korean barbeque restaurants in Korea, but we have chosen two famous restaurants in Hongdae.
They are 고기꾼김춘배 (gogiggunkimchunbae) and 돈주는남자(donjunennamja).

1.고기꾼김춘배 - Gogiggun Kimchunbae

Address: 3, Hongik-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone: 02-6101-3663

Open-time: Everyday 13:00~24:00

Price: Jeju pork belly - 14000won

About 고기꾼김춘배(Gogiggun Kimchunbae):You can enjoy fresh Korean Jeju pork belly also in Seoul.
You don't have to fly all the way to Jeju Island to eat pork belly meat.


2.돈주는남자 - Donjunennamja

Address:34-9, Jandari-ro 6-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:02-6677-0499

Open-time: Everyday 17:00~02:00

Price: Jeju meat grilled with coal(600g) - 32,000won

About 돈주는남자(Donjunennamja):You can enjoy fresh Korean Jeju pork belly also in Seoul.
You don't have to fly all the way to Jeju Island to eat pork belly meat.This place is just right for get-together meal.

119 Songdo Moonhwa-Ro, Yeonsu-Gu, Incheon, Korea 21985
