Bibimbap in Song-Do

Songdo International Business District (Songdo IBD) is a new smart city or "ubiquitous city."
In this page, we will be explaining about bibim-bap places in Songdo.

1.본죽 - Bonjuk

Address:69, Convensia-daero, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon, Korea.

Phone:032-858-6288

Open-time:N/A

Menu/Price:
Abalone porridge - 10,000won

Beef and vegetable porridge - 8,500won

Small octopus and kimchi porridge - 8,000won

About 본죽(Bonjuk):Bonjuk is one of the famous and well-known porridge restaurant in Korea.
Many Koreans enjoy porridge from this place, especially when they are sick.
Koreans have a special culture that if they eat when they are sick, they will recover soon.


2.디오리엔스테이크 - The Orien Steak

Address:126, Convensia-daero, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon, Korea

Phone:032-834-3773

Open-time: Everyday - 11:30~21:30 (Closed on Mondays)

Menu/Price:
Orien steak(Small) - 8,400won

Orien steak(Regular) - 11,900won

Orien steak(Large) - 14,900won

Steak bibim-bap with 5 types of vegetables - 8,000won

About 디오리엔스테이크(The Orien Steak):The Orien Steak restaurant always uses 100% real Korean beef.
Because it is a steak house, you can taste many different steaks. However, you can also order bibim-bap as a side menu.
Many customers look for bibim-bap in this restaurant as well.
