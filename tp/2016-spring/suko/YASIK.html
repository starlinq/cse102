YA-SIK

South Korea's midnight snack culture

Korea's top yasik favorites include: ramyeon, typically eaten with kimchi, chicken and beer, jokbal and bossam, tteokbokki and sundae, gungoguma (roasted sweet potatoes) and hoppang. Kimbap and jokbal is also popular. Past favorites, now less popular, include memilmuk (buckwheat jelly) and chapssaltteok (rice cakes filled with sweet beans).
