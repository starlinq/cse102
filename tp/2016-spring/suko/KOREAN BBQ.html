KOREAN BBQ

Korean method of roasting beef, pork, chicken, or other types of meat

Korean barbecue is often prepared at the dinner's table on gas or charocoal grills that are built into the table itself. Some Korean restaurants that do not have built-in grills provide portable stoves for dinners to use a their tables.
