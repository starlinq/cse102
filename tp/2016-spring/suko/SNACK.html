BUNSIK

food made from flour; inexpensive Korean dishes available at snack restaurants

There are many kinds of Korean Bunsik, such as ddeokbokki, kimbab, and fritters. Ddeokbokki is rice cake cooked with some vegetables, fish cake and spicy red sauce made with gochujang and some sugar. Kimbab is rice rolled with kim, egg, cucumber, ham, and some other ingredients. Fritters are deep fried vegetables, calamari, or shrimp. Tips : You can dip fritters in ddeokbokki sauce.
