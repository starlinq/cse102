Korean BBQ in Gang-Nam

The Gangnam District is one of the 25 gu which make up the city of Seoul, South Korea.
There are many delicious restaurants in Gang-nam as it is the most developed region in Korea.
We would like to introduce to you about two famous Korean BBQ restaurants in Gangnam.

1.투뿔등심 - 2+ Sirloin Steak

Address:6, Teheran-ro 4-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:02-539-8456

Open-time: Everyday 11:30~22:00

Menu/Price:

Aging-eye meat - 37,000won

Galbi - 28,000won

Korean beef fried rice - 20,000won

About 투뿔등심(2+ Sirloin Steak):This restaurant is famous for using Aging-eye technique to tender meat.
It is located near Gangnam station exit 1.Even though the price of the meat is a little more expansive than other Korean BBQ restaurants,
it serves the best Korean beef.

2.봉우화로 - Bongwuhuaro

Address:76, Bongeunsa-ro 18-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:02-558-8452

Open-time: Weekdays 11:30~24:00

Menu/Price:

Beef/150g - 11,000won

삼겹살(pork belly meat)/150g - 11,000won

Galbi/150g - 14,000won

About 봉우화로(Bongwuhuaro):Beef loin, 삼겹살(pork belly meat), cold noodles and cold noodles in a pot are famous menus in Bongwu hua-ro.
