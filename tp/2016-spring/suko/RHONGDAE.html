Bibim-bap in Hongdae

The Hongdae is a region in Seoul, South Korea near Hongik University, after which it is named.
It is known for its urban arts and indie music culture, clubs and entertainments.
Here, in this page, we will introduce some famous 밥집(restaurants with Korean food) of Hongdae.

1.마포나룻집 - Maponarutjip

Address:76-1, Eoulmadang-ro, Mapo-gu, Seoul, Korea.

Phone:02-337-5515.

Open-time: Everyday - 11:30~20:00

Menu/Price:
냉면 (Cold noodles) - 6,500won ~ 13,000won

육회비빔밥 (Raw meat bibim-bap) - 7,000won

About 마포나룻집(Maponarutjip):This restaurant is not only famous for bibim-bap, but also famous for cold noodles.


2.비비리 - Bibiri

Address:29, Dongmak-ro 19-gil, Mapo-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:02-335-0249.

Open-time: Everyday - 11:30~03:00

Menu/Price:Bibim-bap - 5,000won

About 비비리(Bibiri):It is a Korean Bibim-bap restaurant and it is all you can eat!
You can ask for more rice and toppings until you are full without any more charge.

119 Songdo Moonhwa-Ro, Yeonsu-Gu, Incheon, Korea 21985
