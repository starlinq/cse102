Bunsik in Hong-Dae

The Hongdae is a region in Seoul, South Korea near Hongik University, after which it is named.
It is known for its urban arts and indie music culture, clubs and entertainments.
We would like to introduce two bunsik restaurants in Hongdae:또보겠지떡볶이집(Ddobogetji) and 미미네(Mimine).

1.또보겠지떡볶이집 - Ddobogetji

Address:366-14, Seogyo-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul, Korea

Phone:N/A

Open-time: Everyday 11:30~22:00(Closed on Mondays)

Menu/Price:

About 또보겠지떡볶이집(Ddobogetji):At this restaurant, you can create your own ddeokbokki by adding toppings
such as eggs, ramen, cheese rice cake, sausage, shrimps and sweet potatoes.
