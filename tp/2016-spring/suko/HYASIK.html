About 곱소반곱창(Gopsoban Gopchang):This is a restaurant located in Hongdae. It mainly sells Gopchang ( the small intestine of cattle)
which is one of famous nightmeal in Korea. This restaurant is quite well known and was introduced many times on TV.
The price is good and reasonable compared to other restaurants. Also, they offer fried rice after you finish your main dish.
