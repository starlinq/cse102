Bibim-bap in Gang-Nam

The Gangnam District is one of the 25 gu which make up the city of Seoul, South Korea.
There are many delicious restaurants in Gang-nam as it is the most developed region in Korea.
We would like to introduce to you about two famous Korean BBQ restaurants in Gangnam.

1.비비고 - Bibigo

Address: 30, Teheran-ro 1-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Korea.

Phone:02-564-7423

Open-time: Everyday 11:00~22:00

Menu/Price:

Bibigo rice - 8,500won

Bibim-bap - 6,000won

Dolsot bibim-bap - 9,000won

About 비비고(bibigo): Bibigo is a name comprised of 'bibida,'meaning renderin in Korean and 'To-go.'
It is a Korean restaurant in which you can choose from 4 different rices, 6 different toppings and 4 different sauce according to your own taste.
They also provide quick menus such as Tapas.


2.한촌설렁탕 - Hanchon Seollung-tang

Address: 1328-11, Seocho-dong, Seocho-gu, Seoul, Korea.

Phone:02-583-5572

Open-time:

Weekdays - 10:00~22:00

Weekends - 09:00~21:00

Menu/Price:
Seollung-tang - 6,000won

Ox knee soup - 12,000won

About 한촌설렁탕 (hanchon): They have been selling Seollung-tang for 25 years.
100% Beef leg bone is boiled for over 16 hours so that customers can taste deep and clean taste of beef leg bones.
They not only sell Seollung-tang but also 수육 (boiled beef), 냉면 (cold noodles) and other menus.
