Korean BBQ in Song-Do

SONGDO INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS DISTRICT (SONGDO IBD) IS A NEW SMART CITY OR "UBIQUITOUS CITY."
육꼬집(6ggojip) and 애플삼겹살(Apple pork belly house) are the two most famous Korean BBQ restaurants in Songdo.

1.육꼬집 - Yuk ggojip

Address:21-60, Songdo-dong, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon, Korea

Phone:032-851-1231

Open-time: Everyday 16:00~01:00

Menu/Price:

Full pork belly steak - 12,000won

삼겹살(Pork belly meat) - 11,000won

Thin,sliced pork belly meat - 9,000won

About 육꼬집(Yukgoojip):6 ggojip started in 2007, in Songhyun-dong, Incheon.
It is the first restaurant to use big rock hot grill, personal lunch box and fire show on the meat using alcohol.


2.애플삼겹살 - Apple pork belly house

Address:81, Convensia-daero, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon, Korea

Phone:032-834-6092

Open-time: Everyday 16:00~01:30

Menu/Price:

Apple set menu for 2people - 23,900won

Apple set menu for 3-4people(R) - 41,900won

Apple set menu for 3-4people(L) - 46,900won

About 애플삼겹살(Apple Pork):What is special about this restaurant is that it serves pork belly meat with sliced apple and tortillas.
You can also order something to grill as side menus such as shrimp, rice-cake and mushrooms.There is also Fried rice with cheese and scrambled egg.

119 Songdo Moonhwa-Ro, Yeonsu-Gu, Incheon, Korea 21985
